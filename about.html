We Believe that We can Save More Life's with you

Adbere Foundation by tunde is a Nigerian non profit making charity organization. The foundation is committed to providing basic needs, teaching essential life skills, and sharing the Gospel of providing opportunities for women, children, families and communities. By ensuring that it's vision, mission and core values is in top gear and never relenting, the foundation has being catering for several homes, school children and ensuring that more destitute have a place to sleep.
